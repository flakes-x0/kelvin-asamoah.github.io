About Me

Hi! I am Kelvin, a Graduate Civil Engineer passionate about leveraging data science and engineering to tackle transportation and infrastructure challenges. My expertise spans traffic data analysis, transportation planning, traffic safety, and statistical modeling. I have worked on optimizing drone deployment for traffic incident management, developed large-scale data workflows, and collaborated on projects addressing transportation equity and sustainability. Skilled in tools like Python, R, ArcGIS, and PTV Vissim, I enjoy creating innovative solutions for resilient, sustainable systems. I am excited about projects that combine data-driven insights and engineering to make transportation safer and more efficient. Let’s connect and explore how I can contribute!
